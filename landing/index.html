FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you get started. Still curious? Reach out below.

Is PG Sathi available on iOS, Android and Web?+
Yes. The app runs natively on iOS and Android, and the full web app is available at pg-sathi.web.app/app. You can switch between devices anytime — your data stays in sync.
How does payment collection work?+
Tenants pay you directly via UPI to your own bank account. Cash payments can also be recorded manually. Either way, the app auto-generates a PDF invoice and updates rent status.
Is the app available in Hindi?+
Yes — full Hindi and English support across the entire app. Switch languages anytime from settings, and your tenants can view notices and invoices in their preferred language.
Will it work on entry-level Android phones?+
Yes. The app is built mobile-first and is optimised to run smoothly on low-end Android devices with limited RAM and slow networks.
What if I have a very large property?+
PG Sathi Pro covers unlimited beds at a flat ₹499/mo — no tier upgrades, no surprises. For chains or very large operations, reach out via WhatsApp or email and we’ll help you get set up.
Do you help with onboarding?+
Yes. Free setup assistance is included for every property. Reach out on WhatsApp and our team will help you configure rooms, beds, rent plans and tenants so you can go live the same day.
